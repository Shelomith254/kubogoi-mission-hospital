Our Departments

💊 Pharmacy +

Dispenses quality medications and offers expert pharmaceutical care to patients and clinicians.

👶 Maternity & Newborn Unit +

Offers safe and compassionate care for mothers and newborns, including delivery and postnatal services.

🛏 Inpatient (Male, Female, Pediatrics) +

Provides 24-hour admission care for patients across various age groups and medical needs.

🩺 Outpatient Clinic +

Serves walk-in patients for consultation, minor treatment, follow-ups, and referrals.

🩻 Radiology +

Conducts diagnostic imaging services including X-rays, ultrasound, and other essential scans.

🧪 Laboratory +

Performs blood work, pathology, and microbiology for diagnostic and treatment support.

🏥 Comprehensive Care Clinic (CCC) +

Delivers specialized long-term care for patients with chronic conditions like HIV and TB.

👩‍👧 Mother & Child Health (MCH) +

Focuses on prenatal, postnatal, immunization, nutrition, and child wellness services.

💰 Finance Department +

Handles billing, budgeting, insurance processing, and overall financial planning.

🩼 Theatre +

Equipped for both elective and emergency surgeries, maintaining high standards of sterilization and safety.

📋 Administration +

Oversees hospital operations, staff management, and interdepartmental coordination for smooth service delivery.
